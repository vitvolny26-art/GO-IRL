# Vision: GO IRL

## Mission

**Help people spend less time online and more time together.**

## North Star Metric

**Weekly Confirmed Real-Life Meetings**

Not DAU. Not session time. Only meetings that actually happened.

Measurable: "This week, GO IRL organized 487 confirmed meetings."

## Problem

The screen is stealing our lives. We spend hours scrolling through apps looking for things to do, people to meet, places to go. But we rarely actually *do* anything.

## Solution

GO IRL reverses this dynamic:

**Less scrolling. More living.**

GO IRL is a social coordination platform that helps people:

1. **Discover** real-life activities (sports, learning, creative, social)
2. **Connect** with others who share interests
3. **Organize** and coordinate together
4. **Build** trust through real interactions
5. **Create** communities around shared experiences

## Core Principle

**Technology is the bridge. Real life is the destination.**

Real-life experiences are the most valuable thing.

Technology should minimize friction between intention and action.

## What GO IRL Does NOT Do

- It does not replace apps
- It does not compete with social networks
- It does not try to become everything

GO IRL is **focused**: help people organize real-life activities, nothing else.

## Success Metric

**Weekly Confirmed Real-Life Meetings**

Not DAU. Not session time. Not screen time.

Only meetings that actually happened.

## North Star

In 5 years: Millions of real-life activities organized through GO IRL. Strong local communities. Trust as currency. People saying: "I met my best friend through GO IRL."
