# Chapter 1

# Why We Exist

> *"Technology should connect people online only long enough to bring them together offline."*

---

## Introduction

GO IRL was not created because the world needs another application.

The world already has thousands of applications.

Social networks.

Messengers.

Dating services.

Event platforms.

Communities.

Streaming platforms.

Every year more and more digital products compete for one thing:

**Human attention.**

Almost every successful product is optimized for the same metrics.

Increase session time.

Increase retention.

Increase engagement.

Show more content.

Show more ads.

Keep people online.

The longer a person stays inside the application, the more valuable they become.

GO IRL was created with the opposite philosophy.

We do not want to keep people inside the application.

We want to help them leave it.

---

## The Real Problem

People have never been more connected.

And they have never felt more isolated.

We have hundreds of online contacts.

Thousands of followers.

Unlimited entertainment.

Unlimited communication.

Yet millions of people struggle with one surprisingly simple question.

> **"Who wants to do something today?"**

Not next month.

Not after planning for weeks.

Today.

Right now.

The technology to communicate already exists.

The technology to meet each other does not.

GO IRL exists to close that gap.

---

## The World We Believe In

We believe that the best memories never happen inside a phone.

They happen:

on a volleyball court,

during a mountain hike,

over a cup of coffee,

at a concert,

during a board game,

while learning something new,

or simply during an unexpected conversation.

Technology should never replace these moments.

Technology should make them happen.

---

## Our Mission

To help millions of people spend less time scrolling and more time living.

To become the world's leading platform for organizing real-life activities.

To reduce loneliness by making it easier to find people with shared interests.

To help communities grow through real-world interaction.

---

## Our Vision

Imagine a world where every person can answer one simple question:

> **"What can I do with other people today?"**

Open GO IRL.

Choose an activity.

Meet people.

Close the phone.

Live.

That is the future we are building.

---

## What GO IRL Is

GO IRL is not a sports application.

GO IRL is not a dating application.

GO IRL is not a social network.

GO IRL is not an event calendar.

GO IRL is an operating system for real-life activities.

A single platform connecting people through shared experiences.

---

## Our Philosophy

The internet should become invisible.

Its purpose is not to replace reality.

Its purpose is to help people return to reality.

Every successful interaction inside GO IRL should end with the application being closed.

If people continue spending hours inside GO IRL,

we failed.

If people close GO IRL because they are meeting someone in real life,

we succeeded.

---

## Anti Goals

GO IRL will never optimize for:

* endless scrolling;
* addictive mechanics;
* artificial engagement;
* meaningless notifications;
* fake popularity metrics;
* collecting unnecessary personal data.

We reject every feature that increases screen time without increasing real-life interaction.

---

## Our Definition of Success

Traditional products measure:

* Daily Active Users
* Monthly Active Users
* Retention
* Session Length

GO IRL measures something different.

### Real Life Sessions

Every confirmed offline activity means that technology accomplished its purpose.

The application became unnecessary because people met.

That is success.

---

## Core Belief

People don't need another application.

People need another reason to leave home.

GO IRL exists to create those reasons.

Every activity.

Every invitation.

Every event.

Every conversation.

Every friendship.

Every community.

Starts with one simple decision:

**Go in real life.**

---

## The Founder Rule

Every decision made inside GO IRL must answer one question:

> **Does this help people spend more time living and less time scrolling?**

If the answer is no,

the decision is wrong.

No matter how profitable it may be.

---

## Closing Statement

We are not building software.

We are building opportunities.

Opportunities to meet.

To move.

To learn.

To laugh.

To travel.

To create memories.

Technology is only the bridge.

Real life is the destination.

That is why GO IRL exists.
